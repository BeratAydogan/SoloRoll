Effective date: June 10, 2026

SoloRoll ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the SoloRoll mobile application.

We may collect the following types of information:

a. Account Information
If you sign in with Google, we may receive basic account information such as your name, email address, and profile identifier.

b. Gameplay Data
We may store game-related information such as character data, save slots, codex progress, location progress, settings, and other gameplay state needed to operate the app.

c. Purchase Data
If you make in-app purchases, we may receive purchase status and transaction-related information from Google Play in order to unlock content, token packs, remove ads, or provide other purchased features. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

d. Advertising Data
If you view ads, third-party advertising providers such as Google AdMob may collect device and usage-related information to serve, measure, and improve ads.

e. Device and Technical Information
We may collect technical information such as app version, device type, operating system, language preference, crash information, and diagnostic data.

We use collected information to:
- provide and operate SoloRoll

- show rewarded or other in-app ads
- improve app stability, balance, and performance
- respond to support requests
- prevent abuse, fraud, or misuse

7. Data Retention
